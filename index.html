DOGERAI "Dogerai" is a fast-paced head slicing game where players take on the role of a skilled samurai dog who must dodge and slice their way through a barrage of flying MAL heads. With intuitive swipe controls and a variety of challenging game modes, players must use their reflexes and timing to slice as many head as possible while avoiding bombs.
